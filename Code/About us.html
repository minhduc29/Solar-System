About us

We started this website because we believe there should be a more thorough, yet comprehensible website in this field of Astronomy!

Our website aims to provide in-depth and understandable information about the Solar System, which can be beneficial to anyone who wishes to widen their knowledge in this particular area.

Along the way, we have encountered some obstacles from the coding, design procedure to the selecting information process. Nevertheless, we have managed to tackle all the problems to create a useful, informative and aesthetic website that you now see!

We wish to further widen your knowledge about the Solar System, and most importantly, to bring you the passion to discover more about the Solar System, and furthermore, the Universe.

If you wonder why we should learn about the universe, we would put it very simple: our Universe is full of intriguing things to find out! Just imagine there is nothing bigger or older than our Universe!

We would borrow a saying from Stephen Hawking, a very famous English theoretical physicist: “We are just an advanced breed of monkeys on a minor planet orbiting around a very average star. But we can understand the Universe. That makes us something very special”.

We wish you have a great time using our website!

Thank You!
